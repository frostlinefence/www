About Frostline Fence

Local. Family-owned. Built on reputation.

Our Story

Frostline Fence was founded on a simple idea: do the work right, use quality materials, and treat every customer's property like your own. We've been installing fences in this community for years, and we've built our business entirely on referrals and repeat customers.

We're not a national chain. We're your neighbors. When you call us, you talk to the same people who show up to do the work. We take pride in every fence we install because our name is on it: and we have to drive past it.

What We Stand For

  • Honesty. We give you a straight quote and stick to it. No surprise charges at the end of the job.
  • Quality. We only use materials we'd install on our own property. If we wouldn't put it in our yard, we won't put it in yours.
  • Reliability. We show up when we say we will. We finish when we say we will. If something changes, we call you: you don't call us.
  • Clean work. Every job ends with us hauling away all debris. You get a finished fence, not a pile of old posts and wire to deal with.

Licensed & Insured

We are fully licensed contractors and carry liability insurance on every job. You should never hire a fencing contractor who can't say the same.
